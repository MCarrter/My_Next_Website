Pokemon GO! Fanpage!

Pokemon GO is a mobile VR game that encourages players to go outside and play their game! There are over 350 Pokemon currently in the game spanning over 3 Generations!

There have been a ton of features added to the game since launch! Some of these include:

  • A Gym System Rework
  • A Quest System
  • Raid Battles
  • Legendary Pokemon from Raids
  • Trading Between Players
  • A Friend System
  • The Ability to Walk Pokemon
  • Community Days
  • Mythical Pokemon Events
  • And More!

Shiny Pokemon are different-colored versions of Pokemon, with all of them being very difficult to find!

The odds of finding one are 1/450, but with all the shinies out currently along with events increasing odds, they have become easier to find!

There are currently over 100 different Shiny Pokemon in Pokemon GO! With more being added at a rapid pace!

Shiny Krabby and Shiny Kingler was added at the beginning of this month to kick off October! Here is how they look!

What's New Today in Pokemon GO!

Deoxys in Pokemon GO!


The Mythical Psychic-type Pokemon Deoxys has been added into Pokemon GO! In order to obtain this new Pokemon, you must be invited to an EX raid which can be obtained by fighting at EX-compatable gyms by battling Pokemon or participating in public raids.

Pokemon GO Hub created an article where you can learn even more about Deoxys and what Pokemon he is weak to here!
